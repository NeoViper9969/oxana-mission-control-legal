What this is

Oxana Mission Control is a personal tool built and used by its developer, Jonathan Hayes, to plan, review, and publish content for his own channel across the platforms he connects (currently YouTube and TikTok). It runs locally on the developer's own computer and is not a public product or service offered to anyone else.
